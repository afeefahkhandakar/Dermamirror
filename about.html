Dermamirror was created to transform the way young people understand and care for their skin. We realized the issue when I was struggling to keep up with dermatology appointments and taking care of my skin efficiently. The redundancy of going to dermatologists and booking constant appointments is too time consuming during the day when I can be using my time more productively. This caused many issues to not just my own schedule but many others as well. Fortunately this predicament allowed me and my partner to brainstorm. Like the saying goes, to every problem there's a solution— and for us, that became Dermamirror.

Unlike traditional smart mirrors that focuses primarily on facial analysis, Dermamirror provides full-body scan monitoring the progress and tracking designed to support wellness, confidence, and preventative care

Our mission is to make advanced skincare technology accessible, affordable, and emotionally supportive for users everywhere.
